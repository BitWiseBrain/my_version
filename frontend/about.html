Problem Statement

In moments of distress, every second and every action counts. Traditional safety apps are often slow to use, require a screen to be unlocked, and can even escalate a situation if a perpetrator notices the victim reaching for help.

Ankurah solves this through extreme discretion: a safety app disguised as a utility flashlight, allowing for silent, gesture-based distress signaling without drawing attention.
